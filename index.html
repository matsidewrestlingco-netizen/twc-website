Who We Are

Built for Wrestlers.
Built for Winners.

Tiger Wrestling Club is a premier Freestyle & Greco wrestling program in Wexford, Pennsylvania. We develop disciplined, confident athletes through high-quality freestyle & greco wrestling training & elite mindset training.

Whether your athlete is stepping on the mat for the first time or has years of experience, our coaching staff provides the instruction, mentorship, and competitive opportunities to help them reach their full potential.
